Owners of KKR :  The two-time IPL-winning franchise Kolkata Knight Riders is owned by Shah Rukh Khan and Bollywood actress Juhi Chawla and 
		 her husband Jay Mehta (under the banner of Mehta Group).

Owners of SRH :  Sunrisers Hyderabad, who won their maiden IPL title in 2016, are owned by media baron Kalanithi Maran (Sun TV Network).

Owners of KXIP:  The Franchise is owned by Bollywood actress Priety Zinta, Ness Wadia (Wadia Group), 
                 Mohit Burman (Dabur India Ltd.),The Oberoi Group and Karan Paul (Apeejay Surrendra Group).

Owners of CSK :  The 3-times IPL champion Chennai Super Kings is owned by former BCCI President N. Srinivasan’s India Cements. 
				
Owners of DC  :  Earlier known as Delhi Daredevils,The franchise is owned by GM Rao-owned GMR Group. 
	
Owners of MI  :  Mumbai Indians, tge 3 times IPL champion and one of the topmost favourite team

Owners of RR  :  Rajasthan Royals, the first team to win the IPL title way back in 2008, is majorly owned by
